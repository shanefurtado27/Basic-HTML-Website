Steps to make a Pigeon:

1. Fold the paper in half and then unfold it.

2. Fold the paper in half the other way.

3. Fold both layers of paper to the right along the dotted textne.

4. Fold the top layer of paper to the left along the dotted textne.

5. Fold the model in half down along the dotted textne.

6. Fold the top flap of paper up along the dotted textne and then repeat on the other side.

7. Fold the paper along the dotted textne and then unfold it.

8. Make an Inside Reverse Fold using the crease from the previous step.

9. The Pigeon is completed.
